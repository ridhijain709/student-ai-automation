# AI Content Scheduling Automation

## What This Automation Does

Takes a simple Google Sheet content calendar, uses AI to generate captions and post copy, and automatically schedules posts across social media platforms — so businesses maintain a consistent online presence with minimal daily effort.

---

## Business Problem

Small businesses and coaches need consistent social media presence to attract customers, but:

- Creating content daily is time-consuming and inconsistent
- Business owners run out of ideas quickly
- Posting manually means missed days and gaps in brand visibility
- Hiring a social media manager is expensive (₹15,000–₹25,000/month)

---

## Solution Flow

```
Owner fills a few columns in Google Sheet
        ↓
Make.com detects new row
        ↓
AI generates caption from topic + tone + CTA inputs
        ↓
Caption written back to sheet
        ↓
Post scheduled via Buffer / native platform scheduler
        ↓
Owner reviews and approves (optional step)
```

---

## Tools Used

| Tool | Role |
|------|------|
| Google Sheets | Content calendar and input |
| Make.com | Automation trigger and orchestration |
| OpenAI / Gemini API | Caption and post generation |
| Buffer / Meta Business Suite | Scheduling and publishing |
| WhatsApp or Email | Approval notification (optional) |

---

## What Gets Automated

1. **Content ideation support** — AI generates caption options from a topic and tone
2. **Caption writing** — AI drafts posts with correct hashtags and CTAs
3. **Scheduling** — posts queued automatically for the right time
4. **Consistency** — 7–30 days of content planned in one sitting
5. **Brand voice** — consistent tone maintained across all posts

---
