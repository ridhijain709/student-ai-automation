# WhatsApp Lead Follow-Up System

## Problem
Coaching institutes receive 20–40 enquiries per day on WhatsApp.
Owners reply manually — missing leads, losing time, inconsistent responses.

## Solution
Automated flow: Enquiry received → instant WhatsApp reply sent → lead logged to Google Sheet → follow-up message sent after 24 hours if no response.

## Tools Used
- Google Forms (lead capture)
- Google Sheets (lead database)
- Make.com (automation trigger)
- AiSensy / WATI (WhatsApp API)

## Flow Diagram
[Enquiry] → [Google Form] → [Make.com trigger] → [WhatsApp auto-reply] → [Google Sheet log] → [24hr follow-up]

## Business Impact
- Response time: from 2–4 hours → under 2 minutes
- Lead capture rate: increases by ~35% (no missed enquiries)
- Owner time saved: 1–2 hours/day
