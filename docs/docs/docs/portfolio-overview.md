# Portfolio Overview

## Positioning
This repository is a client-facing AI automation portfolio.

It demonstrates practical automations that help businesses:
- capture leads faster
- publish content consistently
- respond to FAQs instantly

---

## Target Clients
- CA firms
- Coaching centres
- Clinics
- Local service businesses
- SMEs
- Small brands

---

## Main Demos

### WhatsApp Lead Follow-up System
Automates lead follow-up after form submission.

### Content Auto-Scheduler
Automates content creation and scheduling.

### FAQ Auto-Reply Bot
Automates responses to repetitive customer questions.

---

## What Makes This Portfolio Useful
This repo is designed to show:
- the business problem
- the automation flow
- the outcome
- the implementation direction
- the proof assets

---

## Recommended Storytelling Format
For each demo, show:
1. Problem
2. Solution
3. Stack
4. Workflow
5. Screenshot
6. Loom video
7. Outcome
