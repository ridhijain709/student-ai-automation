# Sneha Sharma — Demo Script

## Demo Name: Content + Communication Automation Bundle

## Duration: 20 minutes

## Goal: Show that both content creation and client communication can be automated — running in the background while the professional focuses on strategic, high-value work.

---

## Setup Before the Demo

- Content automation demo ready (topic → caption → scheduled post)
- Communication automation demo ready (enquiry → AI reply → lead capture)
- Both demos configured for professional services / personal brand context

---

## Step 1 — Open with the Dual Challenge (2 minutes)

Ask:

> "On a typical week, how much time goes into creating and posting content versus responding to client messages and following up on enquiries? And which one gets neglected when things get busy?"

Let them answer. The common answer is: content gets dropped when clients are busy; communication suffers when content creation takes too long.

Then say:

> "That's the classic professional dilemma — both matter, neither can fully be delegated, and there's not enough time to do both well manually. Let me show you what it looks like when both run automatically."

---

## Step 2 — Content Demo (6 minutes)

Open the content scheduling demo:

> "Here's the content system. You fill in topics for the week — takes 20 minutes. The AI writes the captions, adds hashtags and CTAs, and they're scheduled to go out automatically."

Fill in one row live:

> Topic: "What I've learned about client relationships in the last year"
> Platform: LinkedIn
> Tone: Personal, honest

Show the AI-generated caption:

> "The most important thing I've learned about client relationships is that trust is built in the small moments — not the big deliverables.
>
> When you respond quickly. When you flag a problem before they notice it. When you remember what they mentioned in passing last month.
>
> Clients don't just pay for the work. They pay for the confidence that someone is paying attention.
>
> What's one small thing you do that clients appreciate more than you expected?
>
> #consulting #clientrelationships #professionaldev #businessgrowth"

Say:

> "That post is ready. It'll go out on the scheduled date without you touching your phone."

---

## Step 3 — Communication Demo (6 minutes)

Switch to the communication automation demo:

> "Now here's the communication side. Same principle — the routine parts are automated."

Send a WhatsApp message to the demo number:

> "Hi, I'd like to know what services you offer and what your rates are"

Show the instant AI reply:

> "Hi! Thanks for reaching out. I work with [professional type] on [service description] — typically helping clients with [outcome].
>
> Projects are customised to the brief, with pricing starting from ₹[to be confirmed with client].
>
> The best next step is a quick 20-minute discovery call. Would any time this week or next week work for you?"

Say:

> "That went out in 30 seconds. The lead is captured. The conversation has a clear next step. And you didn't have to stop what you were doing to respond."

---

## Step 4 — Show Both Running Together (2 minutes)

Say:

> "These two systems run in the background simultaneously. Content goes out on schedule. Enquiries are responded to instantly. You check the Google Sheet at the end of the day and see what came in — and the only messages you need to personally respond to are the ones that moved to the next stage."

---

## Step 5 — Close (2 minutes)

> "Practically, this means: your content presence stays consistent even during your busiest months. No one who enquiries goes unanswered. And your time is protected for the work that actually requires you."

Ask:

> "Which of the two feels more urgent to you right now — the content side or the communication side?"

---

## Pricing Conversation

> "You can start with one and add the other later. Each system is ₹8,000–₹12,000 individually. Together as a bundle, it's ₹15,000–₹22,000 — I'd recommend the bundle because they reinforce each other. Content brings in enquiries; the communication system handles them."
