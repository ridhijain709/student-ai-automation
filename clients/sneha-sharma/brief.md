# Sneha Sharma — Client Brief

## Overview

- **Name:** Sneha Sharma
- **Sector:** Professional Services / Consulting / Personal Brand
- **Primary challenge:** Balancing consistent content output and client communication without burning out

---

## Strategic Context

This client profile represents a common professional dilemma: the more successful you become, the more you need to do. Content builds the audience that brings in clients. Client communication serves those clients. But doing both manually becomes unsustainable above a certain level of activity.

The automation solution here is a bundle — two systems working in parallel:
1. Content automation removes the writing and scheduling burden
2. Communication automation handles routine client interactions

Together, they free up several hours per week for higher-value work.

---

## Pain Point Analysis

| Pain Point | Business Impact |
|------------|-----------------|
| Inconsistent content | Audience growth stalls; inbound leads decline |
| Slow client responses | Client confidence drops |
| Time split between content and clients | Neither gets full quality attention |
| No batching system | Everything reactive and in the moment |
| Burnout risk | Unsustainable long-term |

---

## Recommended Automation: Content + Communication Bundle

### Content System
- Weekly content planning in 20 minutes
- AI generates captions for LinkedIn and Instagram
- Posts scheduled and published automatically
- Consistent professional presence without daily effort

### Communication System
- Incoming queries answered automatically
- Routine client questions handled without manual response
- Follow-up sequence for new enquiries
- Escalation for anything that needs personal attention

---

## Implementation Notes

- Assess which system has higher urgency during discovery call
- Build in order of priority; second system can be added within 2 weeks
- Both systems work via Google Sheets + Make.com + WhatsApp/email
- Client provides: service descriptions, content topics list, communication templates

---

## Bundle Positioning

> "Two systems, one setup. Your content goes out consistently. Your clients are responded to promptly. You focus on the work that actually requires you."

---

## Upsell Path

1. Lead capture automation to feed both systems (inbound leads → content + communication pipeline)
2. Analytics: which content drives the most enquiries?
3. Monthly strategy session to review and refresh content pillars
