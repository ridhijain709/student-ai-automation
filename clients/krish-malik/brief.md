# Krish Malik — Client Brief

## Overview

- **Name:** Krish Malik
- **Sector:** Branding / Marketing / Creative Agency
- **Opportunity type:** White-label partnership
- **Strategic value:** Channel partner — one relationship, multiple projects

---

## Strategic Context

Similar to the Ankit Kumar profile, Krish Malik represents an agency partnership opportunity rather than a direct automation sale. The pitch and value proposition are the same: the agency expands its service offering without adding technical headcount, the consultant handles the build, and both parties benefit from the revenue margin.

The key differentiator for Krish may be the creative angle — if the agency works heavily on brand identity and visual content, the content scheduling automation may be more relevant to their clients than a WhatsApp lead flow.

---

## Tailored Pitch Angle

If the agency focuses on brand-building:

> "Your clients invest in branding — but then struggle to maintain consistent content output to bring that brand to life. The content scheduling system ensures their brand stays visible and consistent after your work is done."

If the agency focuses on performance marketing:

> "Your clients generate leads through campaigns — but often lose them after the first contact because there's no follow-up system. The lead automation ensures every lead generated by your campaigns is converted at a higher rate."

---

## Partnership Structure

| Role | Responsibility | Revenue |
|------|---------------|---------|
| Agency | Client relationship, briefing, delivery communication | Margin on each project |
| Consultant | Build, test, handover, initial support | Build fee |

---

## Recommended Starting Point

Offer to do one pilot project together:

> "Pick one of your current clients who needs either lead management or content automation. I'll build it under your brand, you deliver it. We use that to figure out the working model and pricing."

This removes risk and commitment from both sides before a formal partnership is established.

---

## Services to White-Label

- WhatsApp lead follow-up system (ideal for retail, coaching, clinics)
- AI content scheduling system (ideal for brands, D2C, consultants)
- Gmail lead parser (ideal for B2B service companies)

---

## Implementation Notes

- No technical knowledge required from the agency
- Consultant handles all setup, testing, and documentation
- Delivery time: 5–7 days per project
- Agency provides: client brief, business information, branding preferences (if white-labelling UI elements)

---

## Upsell Path

1. Preferred partner arrangement with priority delivery
2. Training materials the agency can use to sell automation to their clients independently
3. Revenue share on monthly retainers from end clients
4. Introductory rate for first 3 projects to establish the working relationship
