# Shubhankar Pathak — Demo Script

## Demo Name: Lead Follow-Up AI System

## Duration: 15 minutes

## Goal: Show that every lead receives an instant response and a structured follow-up sequence automatically — without any manual effort from the team.

---

## Setup Before the Demo

- WhatsApp demo flow with 3-stage follow-up configured
- Google Sheet with lead pipeline visible
- Timer or visual showing the 24-hour and 72-hour follow-up stages

---

## Step 1 — Open with the Problem (2 minutes)

Ask:

> "When a new lead comes in — through WhatsApp, a form, or any other channel — what is the follow-up process right now? Who sends the follow-up, when, and how consistently does it happen?"

Let them describe the current process. Common answers:
- "I follow up when I remember"
- "We have a team member who does it but it's not consistent"
- "We reply once, and if they don't respond, we move on"

Then say:

> "That's one of the most common things I hear. The issue isn't effort — it's that manual follow-up at scale just doesn't hold up. Let me show you what it looks like when every lead is followed up automatically — in the right sequence, at the right time."

---

## Step 2 — Live Demo: Lead Arrives and Is Acknowledged (3 minutes)

Send a WhatsApp message to the demo number:

> "Hi, I'm interested in your services. Please share more details."

Show the instant acknowledgement reply:

> "Hi! Thank you for reaching out. We've received your enquiry and will get back to you with details shortly.
>
> In the meantime, feel free to reply if you have any specific questions — we're happy to help."

Say:

> "That reply went out in under 30 seconds. The lead now knows they've been seen. Their details are being logged. The follow-up clock has started."

---

## Step 3 — Show Lead Capture in Pipeline (2 minutes)

Switch to the Google Sheet:

| Name | Phone | Source | Stage | Last Contact | Status |
|------|-------|--------|-------|--------------|--------|
| Demo Lead | +91-XXXXX | WhatsApp | Stage 0 — Acknowledged | 25 Apr 2026 | Active |

Say:

> "Every lead is tracked here. You can see the stage they're at, when they were last contacted, and what happened."

---

## Step 4 — Show Follow-Up Sequence (4 minutes)

Explain each stage visually or with a diagram:

**Day 1 message:**
> "Hi [Name], just checking in — did you get a chance to think about what we shared? Happy to answer any questions or arrange a quick call."

**Day 3 message:**
> "Hi [Name], wanted to follow up one more time. We've had a few other enquiries for this — if you're still interested, let me know and I'll make sure you have priority."

Say:

> "These go out automatically — no one on your team has to remember. If the lead responds at any point, the automation stops and you get an alert to take over."

---

## Step 5 — Close with Business Outcome (2 minutes)

> "What this means: every lead gets the same treatment — instant reply, two well-timed follow-ups, and a clear escalation if they respond. No lead is forgotten, and your team's time is spent only on people who've shown actual interest."

Ask:

> "How many leads do you currently have that went quiet after the first contact? With this system, those would have received follow-ups automatically."

---

## Pricing Conversation

> "Basic setup — instant reply plus one follow-up — is ₹6,000–₹8,000. Full 3-stage sequence with pipeline tracking is ₹12,000–₹15,000. I can have a working demo tailored to your business within a week."

---

## Objection Handling

**"What if follow-ups annoy the lead?"**
> "The messages are spaced and professional — not pushy. Most people appreciate a follow-up. The ones who find it annoying were probably not going to convert anyway."

**"We already have a CRM."**
> "This can integrate with your CRM or work alongside it. The automation handles the communication — the CRM handles the records. They complement each other."
