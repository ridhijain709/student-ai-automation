# Shubhankar Pathak — Client Brief

## Overview

- **Name:** Shubhankar Pathak
- **Sector:** Sales / Professional Services / Entrepreneurship
- **Primary challenge:** Consistent lead follow-up and pipeline visibility

---

## Strategic Context

Lead follow-up is one of the highest-leverage activities in any business — and one of the most commonly neglected. Studies consistently show that most sales require 5+ follow-up attempts, but most salespeople and business owners stop after 1–2. The reason is not laziness — it's that manual follow-up at scale is genuinely difficult to sustain.

Automating the follow-up sequence ensures:
1. Every lead is treated with equal care
2. Follow-ups happen at the optimal time without manual reminders
3. The sales team's time is spent on warm leads, not chasing cold ones

---

## Pain Point Analysis

| Pain Point | Business Impact |
|------------|-----------------|
| Inconsistent follow-up | Hot leads go cold |
| Manual follow-up drafting | Team time wasted on repetitive messaging |
| No pipeline visibility | Can't tell which stage leads are at |
| Missed follow-up timing | Leads contacted too late or too frequently |
| No record of follow-up history | Hard to know what was communicated |

---

## Recommended Automation: Lead Follow-Up AI System

### Stage 0 — Lead Capture
- Lead submits form or messages on WhatsApp
- Details logged automatically: name, contact, source, timestamp

### Stage 1 — Immediate Acknowledgement (0–5 minutes)
- *"Thank you for reaching out! We've received your enquiry and someone from our team will be in touch shortly."*
- Or more specific based on what the lead enquired about

### Stage 2 — Day 1 Follow-Up
- *"Hi [Name], just wanted to check — did you get a chance to review the information we sent? Happy to answer any questions."*

### Stage 3 — Day 3 Follow-Up
- *"Hi [Name], we've had a couple of other enquiries for the same [service/product]. Wanted to make sure you got priority access if you're interested. Just reply here and we'll sort it out."*

### Stage 4 — Manual Handover
- If lead responds at any stage, the automation stops and alerts the team
- Unresponsive leads flagged for manual review after Stage 3
