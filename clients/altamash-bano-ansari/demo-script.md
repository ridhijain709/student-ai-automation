# Altamash Bano Ansari — Demo Script

## Demo Name: Client Communication AI System

## Duration: 15 minutes

## Goal: Show that routine client communication — responses, updates, reminders — can be handled automatically while maintaining a professional and personal tone.

---

## Setup Before the Demo

- WhatsApp demo with professional services communication flow
- Google Sheet showing client communication log
- Example of a proactive status update message

---

## Step 1 — Open with the Problem (2 minutes)

Ask:

> "How many clients are you currently managing active communication with? And on a typical day, how much time do you spend responding to messages, sending updates, or following up on things that are pending from the client's side?"

Let them answer. Then say:

> "For most professionals I talk to, it's 1–2 hours a day on communication alone. And the frustrating part is that a large portion of that is routine — the same types of queries, the same status updates, the same follow-up chasing. Let me show you what it looks like when those parts are automated."

---

## Step 2 — Live Demo: Client Query Handled Automatically (4 minutes)

Send a WhatsApp message to the demo number:

> "Hi, can you give me an update on where things stand with my project?"

Show the AI reply:

> "Hi [Client Name], thanks for checking in!
>
> Here's a quick update: [milestone completed last week] is done. We're currently working on [current stage] and expect to have [next deliverable] ready by [date].
>
> Is there anything specific you'd like me to clarify or prioritise? Happy to jump on a quick call if helpful."

Say:

> "That response went out in under 30 seconds. The client feels attended to. The response is professional and specific. And no time was taken from the professional's actual work."

---

## Step 3 — Show Communication Log (2 minutes)

Switch to the Google Sheet:

| Client | Message Type | Summary | Date | Status |
|--------|-------------|---------|------|--------|
| [Client A] | Status Query | Update shared: milestone complete | 25 Apr | Responded |
| [Client B] | Document Request | Pending receipt | 24 Apr | Follow-up due |

Say:

> "Every interaction — automated or manual — is logged here by client. You always have a full history without manually tracking anything."

---

## Step 4 — Show Proactive Update (3 minutes)

Say:

> "The system also sends updates proactively — without waiting for the client to ask."

Show a scheduled update message:

> "Hi [Client Name], quick update for the week — [work completed]. We're on track for [deadline]. I'll be in touch by [date] with the next milestone.
>
> Let me know if you have any questions in the meantime."

Say:

> "Clients receive this every week or at key milestones — automatically. They feel informed and confident. And you don't have to remember to send it."

---

## Step 5 — Close (2 minutes)

> "What this system does is handle all the mechanical, repetitive parts of client communication automatically — responses, updates, reminders — so your time and attention go only to the conversations that genuinely need you."

Ask:

> "How many of your current client interactions are routine queries or status checks versus things that genuinely need your expertise?"

---

## Pricing Conversation

> "I can set this up for ₹10,000–₹15,000 depending on the number of communication templates and client volume. Build takes about a week. After that, ongoing support is ₹2,000/month if you want changes made over time."

---

## Objection Handling

**"My clients expect to hear directly from me."**
> "The messages sound like you — because you're the one who approves the templates. Clients see the same professional tone they'd expect from you. The only difference is the response time goes from hours to seconds."

**"What if the context of a query is complex?"**
> "Complex or sensitive queries are flagged and forwarded to you directly. The system handles the routine volume; you handle the edge cases."
