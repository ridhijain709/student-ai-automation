# Anubhav Sharma — Client Brief

## Overview

- **Name:** Anubhav Sharma
- **Sector:** Service Business / Professional Services
- **Primary challenge:** Scaling service business operations without proportional manual effort

---

## Strategic Context

Service businesses — consulting, coaching, creative agencies, professional practices — face a specific scaling challenge: revenue growth requires taking on more clients, but more clients means more manual work in communication, coordination, and delivery management. The solution is to automate the parts of the operation that don't require the expert's judgment — primarily enquiry handling, follow-ups, and routine client communication.

---

## Pain Point Analysis

| Pain Point | Business Impact |
|------------|-----------------|
| Manual enquiry handling | Delayed responses, missed leads |
| Ad hoc client onboarding | Inconsistent experience |
| No follow-up system | Hot leads go cold |
| Manual client updates | Time-consuming, often skipped |
| No visibility on pipeline | Hard to forecast or plan |

---

## Recommended Automation: Service Business Growth System

### Module 1 — Enquiry Handler
- Incoming WhatsApp enquiry triggers instant response
- Reply includes: services overview, starting price range, next step (book call / share details)
- Lead logged automatically

### Module 2 — Follow-Up Engine
- Day 1: follow-up if no response
- Day 3: second follow-up with CTA
- Automation stops on response; team alerted

### Module 3 — Client Onboarding (optional)
- New client confirmed → automated welcome message
- Documents requested automatically
- Kickoff call scheduling message sent

### Module 4 — Project Update Automation
- Weekly or milestone-based update sent to active clients
- No manual drafting required
- Clients feel informed without owner chasing

---

## Implementation Notes

- Discovery call needed to identify highest-priority module
- Build time: 5–7 days per module
- Client provides: services list, pricing context, communication templates
- Works via WhatsApp and/or email

---

## Upsell Path

1. Add referral request automation after successful delivery
2. Monthly reporting sent to clients automatically
3. Lead source tracking to identify which channels generate the best enquiries
