# Anubhav Sharma — Demo Script

## Demo Name: Service Business Growth System

## Duration: 15–20 minutes

## Goal: Show how a service business can handle enquiries, follow-ups, and client communication automatically — without adding headcount or changing the way the team operates.

---

## Setup Before the Demo

- WhatsApp demo with service business enquiry flow
- Google Sheet showing lead pipeline
- Example of a client update message to show in the second half

---

## Step 1 — Discovery (3 minutes)

Ask:

> "What services does your business offer, and where do most of your enquiries come from — WhatsApp, email, social media, or referrals?"

And:

> "When a new enquiry comes in, what happens next? Who responds, how quickly, and what's the follow-up process?"

Use their answers to frame the demo around their specific situation.

---

## Step 2 — Enquiry to AI Reply (4 minutes)

Send a message to the demo number relevant to their services. For example:

> "Hi, I'd like to know about your services and pricing"

Show the instant AI reply:

> "Hi! Thanks for reaching out.
>
> We offer [service category] — typically helping [client type] with [outcome].
>
> Depending on scope, projects start from ₹[X] and are customised to the specific brief.
>
> The best next step is a quick 20-minute discovery call so I can understand what you need and share what's most relevant.
>
> Would [day/time] work for a call? Or feel free to share more about what you're looking for and I'll get back to you."

Say:

> "That response went out in under 30 seconds. Professional, specific to the service, and moves the conversation toward a real conversation. Your team didn't touch it."

---

## Step 3 — Lead Capture and Follow-Up (4 minutes)

Show the lead in the Google Sheet.

Explain the follow-up sequence:

> "If this prospect doesn't reply in 24 hours, the system sends a follow-up automatically. Day 3, another one. If they still don't respond, they're flagged for manual review. If they do respond at any point, the automation stops and you get an alert."

---

## Step 4 — Show Client Update Automation (3 minutes)

Switch to an example of a proactive client update:

> "Once you have active clients, the system can send them regular updates automatically. Something like: 'Hi [Client Name], just a quick update — [milestone] is complete. Next step is [action] by [date]. Let me know if you have questions.'"

Say:

> "This goes out every week or at project milestones. Clients feel looked after. You don't have to remember to send it."

---

## Step 5 — Close (2 minutes)

> "What this system means for your business: every enquiry gets a professional response instantly. Every warm lead gets followed up automatically. And your active clients receive regular updates without manual effort from you. Your time is protected for the work that requires you — not the mechanics of communication."

Ask:

> "What's the biggest communication bottleneck in your business right now — is it more about getting new clients or managing existing ones?"

---

## Pricing Conversation

> "The enquiry handling + follow-up module is ₹8,000–₹10,000. If we add client communication automation, we're looking at ₹15,000–₹20,000 for the full system. Build takes about a week."
