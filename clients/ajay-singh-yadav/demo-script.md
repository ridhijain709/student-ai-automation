# Ajay Singh Yadav — Demo Script

## Demo Name: Admission Conversion AI System

## Duration: 15 minutes

## Goal: Show that every student enquiry is handled instantly, every lead is captured, and follow-ups go out automatically — increasing admission conversion without adding staff.

---

## Setup Before the Demo

- WhatsApp test number with education/admission demo flow active
- Google Sheet open and ready to receive a live entry
- Demo configured for MBA or competitive exam coaching

---

## Step 1 — Open with the Problem (2 minutes)

Ask:

> "During peak admission season, how quickly does your team typically respond to a new WhatsApp enquiry? And how many enquiries a day would you say don't get a reply within the first hour?"

Let them answer. Then say:

> "That delay is where admissions are lost. Students don't wait — they message 4–5 institutes at the same time and go with whoever responds first. Let me show you what it looks like when your institute responds in under a minute — every time."

---

## Step 2 — Live Demo: Enquiry to Instant AI Reply (4 minutes)

Send to the demo number:

> "I want to know about MBA admission"

Show the AI reply arriving within seconds:

---

*Example AI reply:*

> "Hi! Thank you for your interest in our MBA programme. 🎓
>
> Here's a quick overview:
> - Duration: 2 years
> - Eligibility: Any graduation with 50%+
> - Fees: ₹[to be confirmed with institute] per year (easy EMI options available)
> - Next batch starts: [date]
>
> We offer specialisations in Marketing, Finance, and Operations.
>
> Would you like to speak with one of our academic counsellors? You can book a free 30-minute call — just reply with your preferred time or say 'Book call'."

---

Say:

> "That reply went out in under 30 seconds. The student has all the information they need, and there's a clear next step — booking a counselling call."

---

## Step 3 — Show Lead Being Captured (3 minutes)

Switch to the Google Sheet. Show the new row:

| Name | Phone | Course | City | Stage | Date |
|------|-------|--------|------|-------|------|
| Demo Student | +91-XXXXX | MBA | — | New Enquiry | 25 Apr 2026 |

Say:

> "Every enquiry is captured here automatically. Your admissions team can see in real time how many leads are coming in, from which courses, and what stage each one is at."

---

## Step 4 — Show Follow-Up Trigger (3 minutes)

Say:

> "Now — if this student doesn't reply after 24 hours, the system sends a follow-up automatically."

Show the follow-up message:

> "Hi! Just checking in — did you get a chance to go through the MBA programme details? Seats for the upcoming batch are filling up quickly. Would you like to book a quick call with a counsellor?"

Say:

> "This goes out automatically. Your team doesn't have to remember to follow up. The system does it for every lead."

---

## Step 5 — Close with Business Outcome (2 minutes)

Summarise:

> "What you've seen means: every student who enquires about your programmes gets an instant, professional response. They're invited to book a call. Their details are captured. And if they go quiet, a follow-up goes out automatically. One extra admission per month more than pays for the entire system."

Then ask:

> "How many enquiries does your institute typically receive in a day during peak season?"

Use their answer to illustrate ROI:

> "If you're getting 30 enquiries a day and even 3–4 more students book counselling calls because of faster response, that's a direct improvement in conversion."

---

## Pricing Conversation

> "I can build this specifically for your institute at ₹10,000–₹15,000 depending on the number of courses and follow-up stages. Full build takes about a week. You don't need any technical setup — I handle everything and hand over a fully working system."

---

## Objection Handling

**"We already have a counselling team."**
> "This doesn't replace them — it fills their pipeline. Every lead is captured and warmed up before they speak to a counsellor. The team focuses on converting, not answering basic questions."

**"Students might not like talking to a bot."**
> "The experience is a normal WhatsApp conversation. Students respond well when the reply is fast and helpful — they don't need to know it's automated."
