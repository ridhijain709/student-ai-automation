# Ajay Singh Yadav — Client Brief

## Overview

- **Name:** Ajay Singh Yadav
- **Sector:** Education / Coaching / Admissions
- **Primary challenge:** Converting enquiries to admissions at scale, especially during peak season

---

## Strategic Context

Coaching institutes and educational organisations in India operate in a high-competition environment where speed of response directly determines conversion. A student enquiring about an MBA programme typically contacts 3–5 institutes simultaneously. The first institute to reply with clear information and a personal follow-up wins the admission.

This client presents a strong case for an Admission Conversion AI System because:
1. The problem is universally understood in the education sector
2. ROI is immediate — one additional admission covers the entire automation cost
3. The system builds a lead database that has long-term value for the institute

---

## Pain Point Analysis

| Pain Point | Business Impact |
|------------|-----------------|
| Slow response to enquiries | Students choose competing institutes |
| Staff overwhelmed during peak season | Quality of responses drops |
| Repetitive answers to same questions | 1–2 hours of staff time wasted daily |
| No structured lead pipeline | Admissions team has no visibility on conversion rate |
| No follow-up system | Hot leads go cold after initial enquiry |

---

## Recommended Automation: Admission Conversion AI System

### Module 1 — Enquiry Responder
- Instant reply to student WhatsApp queries
- Response includes: course overview, fees, eligibility, batch dates
- CTA: "Book a free counselling call" or "Speak with an advisor"

### Module 2 — Lead Capture
- Student name, phone, course interest, and city logged to Google Sheet
- Timestamp recorded for response time tracking

### Module 3 — Follow-Up Engine
- Day 1 after enquiry: *"Did you get a chance to review the details we shared? Happy to answer any questions."*
- Day 3: *"Seats for the upcoming batch are filling up — would you like to speak with a counsellor?"*
- Stops automatically if student responds

### Module 4 — Counselling Call Booking (optional)
- Student selects a preferred slot
- Confirmation sent via WhatsApp
- Slot logged to calendar or sheet

---

## Implementation Notes

- Client provides: course list, fees, eligibility criteria, batch timings, key selling points
- Build time: 5–7 days
- No coding from client side
- Can be personalised per course type (MBA, UPSC coaching, skill certification, etc.)

---

## Upsell Path

1. Multi-course handling (separate flows for each programme)
2. Post-admission onboarding automation (fee payment reminders, batch joining instructions)
3. Alumni referral flow (automated reach-out to past students for referrals)
