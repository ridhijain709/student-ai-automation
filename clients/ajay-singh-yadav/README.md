# Ajay Singh Yadav — Admission Conversion AI System

## Business Context

Ajay Singh Yadav is likely associated with a coaching institute, educational institution, or professional training setup. Educational businesses in India face intense competition for student admissions, with enquiries peaking during admission seasons and requiring fast, consistent responses to convert prospects before they approach competitors.

## Pain Point Summary

- Admission enquiries arrive on WhatsApp, email, and phone simultaneously
- Staff struggle to respond quickly during peak season
- Delayed replies cause students to choose competing institutes
- Same questions asked repeatedly: fees, eligibility, batch timings, placements
- No structured lead tracking — admissions team doesn't know who enquired, when, or what stage they're at

## Recommended Automation Offer

**Admission Conversion AI System**

A WhatsApp-first automation that replies instantly to student enquiries, shares course details and fees, captures lead information, and triggers follow-up reminders to maximise conversion.

## What the Demo Should Show

1. A WhatsApp message arrives: *"Tell me about MBA admission"*
2. AI replies instantly with program overview, fees, eligibility, and a call-booking CTA
3. Lead is logged to Google Sheet with name, course interest, and contact
4. A follow-up message is shown going out the next day to leads who haven't responded
5. Brief explanation: every enquiry is handled instantly, no lead goes cold
