# Ankit Kumar — Client Brief

## Overview

- **Name:** Ankit Kumar
- **Sector:** Branding / Marketing / Digital Agency
- **Opportunity type:** Partnership (white-label), not direct client
- **Primary value:** Agency acts as channel to multiple end clients

---

## Strategic Context

Partnering with an agency multiplies leverage. One agency relationship can generate multiple automation projects across their client base without requiring individual outreach to each end client. The pitch is different from a direct client pitch — it's a business development conversation, not a solution pitch.

The value proposition to the agency is:
1. They can offer a new service without hiring technical staff
2. Their clients get more value, improving retention
3. They earn a margin on every automation project
4. The arrangement is non-exclusive and low-commitment to start

---

## Partnership Structure

### Revenue Model

| Role | Amount |
|------|--------|
| End client pays agency | ₹20,000–₹30,000 |
| Agency pays consultant | ₹8,000–₹12,000 |
| Agency margin | ₹10,000–₹18,000 |

### Delivery Model

1. Agency client requests automation solution
2. Agency briefs consultant on scope and client type
3. Consultant builds and tests the system (5–7 days)
4. Delivered under agency's brand / white-label
5. Agency maintains client relationship and support communication

---

## What to Offer as a Starting Point

Suggest a pilot project with one of their existing clients:

> "Pick one of your current clients who has a WhatsApp or lead management problem. I'll build the automation, you deliver it under your agency's name. Let's see how it goes and take it from there."

This removes risk from the agency and lets them test the model before committing.

---

## Services to White-Label

- WhatsApp lead follow-up system
- Gmail lead parsing and logging
- AI content scheduling
- FAQ and enquiry bot for any business type

---

## Implementation Notes

- No technical involvement required from the agency
- Consultant handles all build and setup
- Agency briefs scope, provides client's business information
- Delivery documentation and client handover materials provided by consultant

---

## Long-Term Potential

A single agency partnership with 5–10 active clients could generate:
- 2–4 automation projects per quarter
- Recurring revenue from monthly support
- Referrals from the agency's network

---

## Upsell Path

1. Priority build slots for the agency's clients
2. Training for the agency's team to oversee automations independently
3. Revenue-share on monthly retainer from end clients
