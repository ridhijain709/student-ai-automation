# Ankit Kumar — White-Label AI Automation Partnership

## Business Context

Ankit Kumar is likely running a branding, marketing, or digital agency. Agencies at the growth stage are increasingly being asked by their clients for automation and AI solutions that complement their core services — but most agencies don't have in-house automation capability. This creates a partnership opportunity.

## Pain Point Summary

- Clients ask for automation and AI tools after branding/marketing work is done
- Agency cannot currently offer automation — risks losing clients to larger full-service firms
- Building automation in-house requires technical investment the agency may not want to make
- Missed revenue opportunity: automation projects are high-margin and recurring

## Recommended Automation Offer

**White-Label AI Automation Partnership**

A revenue-sharing arrangement where the automation is built and delivered under the agency's brand. The agency sells it to their clients, keeps the margin, and the backend build is handled by the consultant.

## What the Demo Should Show

1. Show a sample client workflow (e.g., a branding client asking for WhatsApp lead automation)
2. Show how the system would be delivered under the agency's branding
3. Demonstrate the turnaround: client briefs agency → agency briefs consultant → system delivered in 5–7 days
4. Walk through the revenue model: agency marks up the build and earns the margin
