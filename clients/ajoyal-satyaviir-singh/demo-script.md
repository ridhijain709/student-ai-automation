# Ajoyal — Demo Script

## Demo Name: Distributor Growth System for Ajoyal

## Duration: 15–20 minutes

## Goal: Show that distributor enquiries can be handled, logged, and followed up automatically — without any manual effort from the Ajoyal team.

---

## Setup Before the Demo

- WhatsApp test number with demo flow active
- Google Sheet open and empty (ready to receive a live entry)
- Demo prompt configured for a food brand (distributor enquiry)

---

## Step 1 — Open with the Problem (2 minutes)

Ask Satyaviir one question:

> "Right now, when a new distributor or stockist messages you on WhatsApp asking about your products or pricing — what happens next?"

Let them describe their current process. Common answers:
- "I reply manually when I see it"
- "Someone from my team handles it but it's inconsistent"
- "We miss a lot of them honestly"

Then say:

> "That's exactly the problem I built this system for. Let me show you what it looks like when every enquiry is handled instantly — with zero manual effort."

---

## Step 2 — Live Demo: Enquiry to AI Reply (5 minutes)

Send the following WhatsApp message to the demo number:

> "I want to become a distributor for your products"

Show the AI reply arriving within seconds. The reply should include:
- A professional greeting
- Brief intro to Ajoyal's product categories
- MOQ information
- Next step: *"Please share your name, city, and current distribution business so we can check availability in your area."*

Say:

> "This reply went out in under 30 seconds. Your team didn't touch it."

---

## Step 3 — Show the Lead Being Captured (3 minutes)

Switch screen to the Google Sheet.

Show the new row that was just created:

| Name | Phone | City | Enquiry | Timestamp | Status |
|------|-------|------|---------|-----------|--------|
| Demo User | +91-XXXXX | (blank) | Distributor enquiry | 25 Apr 2026, 10:32 AM | New |

Say:

> "Every enquiry is logged here automatically. You can filter by city, product interest, or status. No one has to manually enter anything."

---

## Step 4 — Show the Follow-Up Logic (3 minutes)

Explain the follow-up trigger:

> "If this distributor doesn't reply within 24 hours, the system sends a follow-up automatically. Something like: 'Hi, just following up — we have availability in your region and would love to connect. Can you share your details?'"

If possible, show the Make.com flow diagram or a screenshot of the automation trigger.

Say:

> "Right now, follow-ups probably depend on someone remembering. This system makes sure no distributor lead goes cold."

---

## Step 5 — Close with the Business Outcome (2 minutes)

Summarise:

> "What we just walked through means: every distributor who messages you gets an instant professional response, their details are logged, and if they don't reply, a follow-up goes out automatically. You don't need to add staff or change your workflow — it runs in the background."

Then ask:

> "Does this fit the kind of distributor communication Ajoyal is doing right now?"

---

## Pricing Conversation

If they're interested:

> "I can build this specifically for Ajoyal at ₹6,000 as an introductory setup. Once it's working and you're happy with it, we can expand to track distributor stages, integrate with email for formal onboarding, or cover other product lines. The full build would be ₹12,000–₹18,000."

---

## Objection Handling

**"We already have someone doing this."**
> "That's great — this system supports them rather than replacing them. It handles the repetitive acknowledgement and logging so they can focus on actual conversations with serious distributors."

**"We don't have a WhatsApp Business API yet."**
> "I can help you set that up as part of the project. It's a straightforward process — most setups are done in a day."

**"What if the AI gives a wrong answer?"**
> "All replies are based on the exact information you give me — product list, pricing range, MOQ. It only says what you tell it to say. Anything outside that is escalated to your team."
