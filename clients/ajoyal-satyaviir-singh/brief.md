# Ajoyal — Client Brief

## Company Overview

- **Brand:** Ajoyal
- **Founder:** Satyaviir Singh
- **Sector:** FMCG / Food Brand
- **Growth stage:** Expanding distributor and stockist network
- **Primary challenge:** Scaling distributor communication without adding headcount

---

## Strategic Context

Food brands at the growth stage face a recurring problem: the founder or a small team is the bottleneck for distributor communication. Every new stockist enquiry requires a manual response, and follow-ups get missed as the business scales. The window to convert a distributor prospect is often short — if a competitor responds faster, the opportunity is lost.

Automating this function does not replace the relationship — it ensures that every enquiry is acknowledged instantly and that no lead goes cold due to a missed follow-up.

---

## Pain Point Analysis

| Pain Point | Business Impact |
|------------|-----------------|
| Manual WhatsApp replies to distributors | Owner or team time consumed daily |
| Inconsistent responses | Brand perception weakened with serious distributors |
| No structured onboarding flow | New stockists take longer to activate |
| Missing follow-ups | Distributor pipeline shrinks without new additions |
| No lead tracking | No visibility on who enquired, from where, and their status |

---

## Recommended Automation: Distributor Growth System

### Module 1 — Enquiry Handler
- Incoming WhatsApp message triggers an instant reply
- Reply includes: product categories, MOQ, area availability, next step (share details or book call)
- Fallback: escalate to human if query is complex

### Module 2 — Lead Logger
- Every enquiry logged to Google Sheet automatically
- Columns: Name, City, Phone, Enquiry Type, Stage, Last Message Date

### Module 3 — Follow-Up Engine
- If no response in 24 hours: automated follow-up message sent
- If still no response in 48 hours: flag for manual review

### Module 4 — Status Tracker (optional)
- Sheet updated as conversation progresses
- Owner can filter by city, product category, or status

---

## Implementation Notes

- System can be built on Make.com + WATI or AiSensy
- No code required from the client — delivered as a ready-to-use setup
- Initial build takes 5–7 days including testing
- Client provides: WhatsApp business number, product list, MOQ details
- First demo can be shown with a generic food brand prompt — personalised to Ajoyal for the actual pitch

---

## Competitive Angle

Most food brand founders handle distributor communication personally. This system positions the consultation as *"giving back your time while making sure every distributor opportunity is captured"* — not as a tech product.
