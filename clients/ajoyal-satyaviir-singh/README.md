# Ajoyal — Distributor Growth System

## Business Context

Ajoyal is a food brand founded by Satyaviir Singh. The business operates in the FMCG space and relies on a distributor and stockist network for growth. Building and managing that network requires consistent communication — responding to enquiries, onboarding new distributors, and following up with prospects.

## Pain Point Summary

- Distributor enquiries arrive on WhatsApp with no structured response system
- Manual replies from the owner or team cause delays and inconsistency
- New stockist onboarding is done ad hoc — no standard process
- Follow-ups on distributor leads are often missed
- No central record of who has enquired, their city, or their current status

## Recommended Automation Offer

**Distributor Growth System**

A WhatsApp-based automation that handles distributor and stockist enquiries automatically, logs every lead, and sends follow-up messages to prospects who haven't responded.

## What the Demo Should Show

1. A WhatsApp message arrives: *"I want to become a distributor"*
2. AI replies instantly with product category, MOQ, and next steps
3. Google Sheet updates with lead name, city, and enquiry details
4. A follow-up message is shown triggering after 24 hours with no response
5. Brief explanation: every distributor enquiry is now captured and followed up without manual effort
