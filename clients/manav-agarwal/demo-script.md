# Manav Agarwal — Demo Script

## Demo Name: Business Operations AI System

## Duration: 20 minutes

## Goal: Identify the biggest operational pain point through a discovery conversation, then demonstrate a relevant automation that solves it.

---

## Note on This Demo

Unlike other client demos, this one begins with discovery rather than a specific demo flow. The goal of the first 5–7 minutes is to understand Manav's actual pain point before showing a solution. This increases relevance and conversion significantly.

---

## Setup Before the Demo

- All three core demos ready: WhatsApp lead flow, Gmail parser, content scheduler
- One generic operations demo that shows lead capture + logging as a default
- Willingness to say: "Let me build a version specific to what you just described" rather than forcing a generic demo

---

## Step 1 — Discovery: Understand the Pain Point (7 minutes)

Open with:

> "Before I show you anything, I want to understand what's actually costing you time right now. I'd rather show you something relevant than something generic."

Ask:

1. "What does your day typically involve — are you mostly managing people, clients, orders, or a mix?"
2. "Which part of operations is the most manual and repetitive?"
3. "Do you have a WhatsApp number or email inbox where enquiries or client messages come in?"
4. "How do you currently track what's happening — spreadsheet, CRM, something else?"

Take notes. Based on their answers, select the most relevant demo:

- **WhatsApp-heavy business → Show WhatsApp lead flow demo**
- **Email-heavy business → Show Gmail parser demo**
- **Content/brand-building → Show content scheduler demo**

---

## Step 2 — Pivot to Relevant Demo (8 minutes)

Say:

> "Based on what you've described, the most relevant thing I can show you is [specific demo]. Let me walk you through it."

Run the relevant demo, connecting every step back to the pain point they described.

Example bridge:

> "You mentioned your team manually responds to every WhatsApp enquiry. Here's what that looks like when it's automated..."

---

## Step 3 — Quantify the Impact (3 minutes)

After the demo, ask:

> "Roughly how many of these [enquiries / emails / tasks] does your team handle per day?"

Use their number to calculate impact:

> "If each one takes 3–4 minutes to handle manually, that's [X hours] per day. This system handles it in seconds. That's [Y hours] returned to your team every week."

---

## Step 4 — Close (2 minutes)

> "I can build a version of this specifically for your business — using your team's workflow, your product or service details, your current tools. It takes about a week to deliver and doesn't require any technical work from your side."

Ask:

> "Does this feel like the kind of problem it would be worth solving right now?"

---

## Pricing Conversation

> "First automation module: ₹8,000–₹12,000 depending on complexity. If we identify 2–3 processes to automate, a bundled approach comes to ₹20,000–₹30,000 with priority delivery."

---

## Objection Handling

**"We're not sure what to automate yet."**
> "That's exactly what a discovery call is for. Give me 20 minutes and I'll tell you the 2–3 highest-impact things to automate in your business — no commitment needed."

**"We've tried automation tools before and they were too complex."**
> "What I deliver is fully set up and handed over — you don't need to learn any tool. I configure everything, test it, and walk you through using it."
