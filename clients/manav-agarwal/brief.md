- **Name:** Manav Agarwal

---

## Strategic Context

Manav's profile suggests a growing business or entrepreneurial venture where operational inefficiency is becoming a bottleneck. The automation opportunity here depends on a discovery conversation — the goal of the initial outreach is not to pitch a specific product but to understand where time is being lost.

Once the core pain point is identified, the solution is straightforward: automate that specific process first, demonstrate clear ROI, and build from there.

---

## Discovery Questions to Ask

Before pitching any specific automation, understand:

1. What does a typical day look like for you and your team?
2. Which tasks do you or your team spend the most time on that feel repetitive?
3. Do you have a WhatsApp or email channel where leads or clients reach out?
4. How do you currently track leads, orders, or customer interactions?
5. What would be most valuable to you — saving time, reducing errors, or better visibility on the business?
