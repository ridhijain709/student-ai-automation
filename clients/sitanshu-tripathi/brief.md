# Sitanshu Tripathi — Client Brief

## Overview

- **Name:** Sitanshu Tripathi
- **Sector:** Consulting / Professional Services / Personal Brand
- **Primary challenge:** Maintaining consistent, high-quality social media presence without daily time investment

---

## Strategic Context

For professionals and consultants, content consistency on LinkedIn and Instagram is directly tied to lead generation and credibility. A professional who posts regularly attracts inbound enquiries; one who doesn't gets forgotten. But the time required to think of topics, write captions, add hashtags, and schedule posts daily is genuinely prohibitive for a busy professional.

The automation case is strong because:
1. The task (caption writing) is highly automatable with AI
2. The output quality from AI is good enough for professional posts
3. The client only needs to provide a topic — the rest is handled
4. Time savings are immediate and measurable

---

## Pain Point Analysis

| Pain Point | Business Impact |
|------------|-----------------|
| Inconsistent posting | Credibility and visibility drop |
| Time-consuming content creation | Pulls focus from core work |
| No content bank | Can't post when busy |
| Reactive content | No strategic messaging |
| No scheduling system | Posts forgotten under workload |

---

## Recommended Automation: AI Content Scheduling System

### Module 1 — Content Calendar Input
- Client fills a Google Sheet with: date, topic, platform, tone
- Takes 15–20 minutes to plan a week of content

### Module 2 — AI Caption Generator
- Make.com trigger fires on new row
- Gemini or OpenAI generates a full caption: hook, body, CTA, hashtags
- Caption written back to the sheet for review

### Module 3 — Review and Approval (optional)
- Client reviews captions in the sheet
- Marks approved posts with a flag column
- Only approved posts proceed to scheduling

### Module 4 — Auto-Scheduling
- Approved captions sent to Buffer or Meta Business Suite
- Posts go out at pre-set optimal times
- Platform handles actual publishing

---

## Implementation Notes
