# Priyanshi Rawat — Client Brief

## Overview

- **Name:** Priyanshi Rawat
- **Sector:** Personal Brand / Content Creation / Professional Services
- **Primary challenge:** Consistent content output without disproportionate time investment

---

## Strategic Context

Personal branding on LinkedIn and Instagram has become a meaningful driver of professional opportunities — consulting clients, job offers, speaking engagements, and community-building. But maintaining consistent, quality content is difficult for a professional who is also managing core work, client relationships, and other responsibilities.

The automation opportunity here is high because:
1. Content writing is the most time-consuming step and is highly automatable with AI
2. Scheduling is already possible with existing tools — the system just needs to be connected
3. The client only needs to provide ideas and direction — the system handles execution

---

## Pain Point Analysis

| Pain Point | Business Impact |
|------------|-----------------|
| Inconsistent posting | Audience growth stalls |
| Time-consuming content writing | Delays and missed posting days |
| No content bank | Nothing to post during busy weeks |
| Manual scheduling | Posts forgotten |
| No system | Everything depends on motivation and memory |

---

## Recommended Automation: Personal Brand Content Engine

### Module 1 — Content Topic Input
- Weekly 15–20 minute session to fill content calendar
- Fields: date, platform, topic, tone, CTA
- No writing required — just ideas and direction

### Module 2 — AI Caption Generator
- Triggered on each new row
- Generates hook, body, CTA, hashtags
- Platform-appropriate format (LinkedIn long-form vs Instagram short-form)
- Caption written back to sheet for review

### Module 3 — Tone Customisation
- First batch: review and give feedback on tone
- System adjusted to match personal voice
- After 2–3 rounds: output reads authentically

### Module 4 — Auto-Scheduling
- Approved posts sent to Buffer
- Scheduled for optimal posting times
- Posts published automatically

## Implementation Notes

- Build time: 3–5 days
- First content batch generated during the demo session
- Tone calibration in 2–3 iterations
- Works for LinkedIn and Instagram (or both)

---

## Upsell Path

1. Monthly content strategy session (30 minutes) + batch generation for the month
2. Repurpose LinkedIn posts for Instagram Reels scripts or short video outlines
3. Email newsletter using the same content pipeline
4. Analytics review: which content types perform best
