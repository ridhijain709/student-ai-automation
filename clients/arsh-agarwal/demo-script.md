# Arsh Agarwal — Demo Script

## Demo Name: Restaurant and Enquiry AI System

## Duration: 15 minutes

## Goal: Show that a restaurant never misses a booking request or customer query — even during peak service or after closing time.

---

## Setup Before the Demo

- WhatsApp demo number with restaurant flow active
- Google Sheet open to show booking log
- Demo configured with a sample menu, opening hours, and seating capacity

---

## Step 1 — Open with the Problem (2 minutes)

Ask:

> "During a busy dinner service, what typically happens when a WhatsApp message comes in asking about table availability? Does someone check it immediately, or does it wait?"

Let them describe the reality. Common answers:
- "It waits — we're focused on the floor"
- "Someone checks it eventually but not always fast enough"
- "We miss some and then the customer has already gone elsewhere"

Then say:

> "That's the window where bookings are lost. Let me show you what it looks like when every enquiry is handled instantly — even at 8 PM on a Saturday."

---

## Step 2 — Live Demo: Booking Request (5 minutes)

Send to the demo number:

> "Hi, can I book a table for 4 on Saturday evening around 7:30?"

Show the AI reply:

> "Hi! Thanks for reaching out. 🙏
>
> Saturday evening at 7:30 PM for 4 guests — let me check availability.
>
> Great news — we have a table available! Could I confirm your name and a contact number? We'll hold the booking for 15 minutes at the time of arrival.
>
> Looking forward to having you!"

Show the booking being logged in the Google Sheet:

| Name | Party Size | Date | Time | Phone | Status |
|------|-----------|------|------|-------|--------|
| Demo Guest | 4 | Saturday | 7:30 PM | — | Confirmed |

Say:

> "That booking was confirmed in under a minute. Your team didn't touch it. The booking is in the sheet. The customer is happy."

---

## Step 3 — Menu and FAQ Demo (3 minutes)

Send:

> "Do you have a vegetarian menu? What are your prices like?"

Show the AI reply:

> "Yes, we have an extensive vegetarian menu! 🌿
>
> We offer everything from classic Indian vegetarian dishes to continental options.
>
> Our mains range from ₹250–₹600, and we have set meal options starting from ₹450 per person.
>
> Would you like to see our full menu, or shall I answer any specific questions?"

Say:

> "Common questions — menu, pricing, dietary options — are all handled automatically. Staff focus on the guests who are already in the restaurant."

---

## Step 4 — After-Hours Scenario (2 minutes)

Say:

> "Now let's say this message comes in at 11 PM, after the restaurant is closed."

Send:

> "Hi, I'd like to book a table for Sunday lunch for 6 people"

Show the AI reply:

> "Hi! Thanks for reaching out. We're closed right now, but your booking request has been noted. ✅
>
> Our team will confirm the table for Sunday lunch first thing tomorrow morning.
>
> We'll need: your name, preferred time, and a contact number. Feel free to reply now and we'll be in touch!"

Say:

> "The customer is acknowledged. The request is logged. Your team sees it first thing in the morning. No one goes unanswered."

---

## Step 5 — Close (2 minutes)

> "What this means: every booking request is handled instantly — during service, after hours, whenever. No missed tables. No customers going elsewhere because they didn't hear back. And your staff focus on the guests in front of them, not the phone."

Ask:

> "How many bookings would you estimate come in through WhatsApp in a typical week? And how many do you think are currently being missed?"

---

## Pricing Conversation

> "Basic setup — booking handling and FAQ responses — is ₹8,000–₹10,000. Full system with delivery queries, after-hours handling, and booking log is ₹12,000–₹15,000. I can have it live in about a week."

---

## Objection Handling

**"We already have someone managing WhatsApp."**
> "This supports them — they're freed up to handle the guests in the restaurant rather than the phone. The routine messages are handled automatically; anything that needs a personal touch is flagged."

**"What if someone makes a special request the AI can't handle?"**
> "Special requests are flagged for staff. The system handles the standard 80% — availability, menu, hours, delivery. Anything specific gets routed to a human."
