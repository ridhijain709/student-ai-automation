# Arsh Agarwal — Client Brief

## Overview

- **Name:** Arsh Agarwal
- **Sector:** Restaurant / F&B / Hospitality
- **Primary challenge:** Managing customer communication and bookings without missing opportunities during busy service periods

---

## Strategic Context

Restaurants and cafés lose business in two predictable ways:
1. A customer sends a WhatsApp message asking about availability or the menu, the message sits unread during service, and by the time someone replies, the customer has booked elsewhere.
2. After-hours enquiries receive no response until the next day — too late.

Both problems are solved with a WhatsApp automation that handles routine customer interactions instantly, regardless of how busy the team is or what time of day it is.

---

## Pain Point Analysis

| Pain Point | Business Impact |
|------------|-----------------|
| Missed booking requests | Lost revenue per table |
| Repetitive WhatsApp queries | Staff time during service |
| Inconsistent responses | Poor customer experience |
| After-hours silence | Customers book competitors |
| No centralised booking record | Double bookings, confusion |

---

## Recommended Automation: Restaurant and Enquiry AI System

### Module 1 — Booking Handler
- Customer asks about availability
- AI collects date, time, party size
- Confirms availability or suggests alternatives
- Booking logged to Google Sheet
- Confirmation message sent

### Module 2 — Menu and FAQ Responder
- Common questions answered instantly: menu options, dietary requirements, pricing, opening hours
- For special requests: notes taken and flagged for staff

### Module 3 — Delivery Area Checker
- Customer asks about delivery
- AI confirms available areas and delivery timing
- Order directed to appropriate channel

### Module 4 — After-Hours Handler
- Late-night or early-morning messages receive a professional response
- Booking requests queued for next morning confirmation
- Customer is not left waiting without acknowledgement

---

## Implementation Notes

- Client provides: menu summary, opening hours, delivery areas, seating capacity, booking rules
- Build time: 5–7 days
- Works with existing WhatsApp Business number
- Google Sheet serves as the reservations log; can be shared with front-of-house staff

---

## Upsell Path

1. Loyalty message automation (regular customers receive special offers)
2. Event and private dining enquiry handling
3. Post-visit follow-up (review request or return visit offer)
4. Integration with a formal reservation system if they use one
