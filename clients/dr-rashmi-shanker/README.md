## Business Context

Dr. Rashmi Shanker is a medical professional (likely a dermatologist or general practitioner) who manages both patient consultations and clinic administration. - Repetitive patient queries consume doctor and staff time daily
- After-hours messages go unanswered, causing patients to look elsewhere
