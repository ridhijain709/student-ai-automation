# Dr. Rashmi Shanker — Demo Script

## Demo Name: Patient Handling AI System

## Duration: 15 minutes

## Goal: Show that a clinic can handle routine patient queries, appointment requests, and after-hours messages automatically — without manual effort from the doctor or staff.

---

## Setup Before the Demo

- WhatsApp test number with clinic demo flow active
- Google Sheet open to show appointment capture
- Demo configured with dermatology-specific responses (hair fall, acne, skin treatments)

---

## Step 1 — Open with the Problem (2 minutes)

Start with a question:

> "How many WhatsApp messages does your clinic receive in a day, roughly? And how many of them are asking about the same things — costs, treatments, appointment timings?"

Let them respond. Then say:

> "That's exactly the situation I designed this system for. Let me show you what it looks like when those queries are handled automatically — and every patient still gets a proper, professional response."

---

## Step 2 — Live Demo: Patient Query to AI Reply (4 minutes)

Send the following WhatsApp message to the demo number:

> "What is the cost of hair fall treatment?"

Show the AI reply arriving within seconds:

---

*Example AI reply:*

> "Hi! Thank you for reaching out to our clinic.
>
> Hair fall treatment depends on the underlying cause — we offer PRP therapy, mesotherapy, and personalised treatment plans based on your diagnosis.
>
> Starting consultations are typically ₹X and above, and treatment plans are recommended after an initial assessment with Dr. Rashmi.
>
> Would you like to book an appointment? Please share your preferred date and time and we'll confirm availability."

---

Say:

> "That response took under 30 seconds. Your staff didn't touch it. The patient gets a professional answer, and there's a clear next step."

---

## Step 3 — Show Appointment Capture (3 minutes)

Now send:

> "I'd like to book an appointment for Saturday"

Show the AI asking for Name and preferred time, then confirm the appointment logging in the Google Sheet.

Switch to the sheet and show the row:

| Patient Name | Phone | Treatment Interest | Preferred Date | Status |
|--------------|-------|-------------------|----------------|--------|
| Demo Patient | +91-XXXXX | Hair fall | Saturday | Pending Confirmation |

Say:

> "Every appointment request goes here automatically. No double-entry, no missed requests."

---

## Step 4 — Show After-Hours Handling (2 minutes)

Say:

> "Now imagine this message comes in at 9 PM — after your clinic has closed."

Send:

> "I have a lot of acne suddenly — very worried, can I see the doctor?"

Show the AI responding professionally:

> "Hi, thank you for reaching out. Our clinic is currently closed, but your query has been noted. Dr. Rashmi's team will be in touch with you first thing tomorrow morning to help schedule an appointment. If this is a medical emergency, please contact your nearest emergency care facility."

Say:

> "The patient is reassured. The query is logged. Your team sees it first thing in the morning. No patient feels ignored."

---

## Step 5 — Close with Business Outcome (2 minutes)

Summarise:

> "What you've just seen means: every patient who messages your clinic gets an instant, professional response — whether it's during hours or after hours. Routine queries are handled automatically. Appointment requests are logged. Your team and your time are protected for the patients who genuinely need direct attention."

Then ask:

> "Does this fit the kind of patient communication your clinic is managing right now?"

---

## Pricing Conversation

> "I can build a version of this specifically for your clinic — using your treatment list and your pricing — for ₹8,000 to ₹12,000 depending on the scope. If you want appointment booking integration and after-hours handling included, we're looking at ₹12,000–₹18,000. I can have a working demo ready within a week of receiving your clinic details."

---

## Objection Handling

**"What if the AI gives medically incorrect information?"**
> "The system only states what you tell it to state — treatments you offer, pricing ranges you provide. Anything clinical or diagnostic is always escalated to the doctor. There's no AI diagnosing patients."

**"My patients aren't very tech-savvy."**
> "The patient experience is identical to a normal WhatsApp conversation. They don't know it's automated — they just get a fast, professional reply."

**"We already have a receptionist."**
> "This supports the receptionist — they no longer have to answer the same questions 15 times a day. They focus on the patients who actually need personal attention."
