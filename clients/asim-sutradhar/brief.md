---

## Strategic Context

Asim's tech background means the pitch needs to be different. He won't be impressed by a basic WhatsApp bot demo. The conversation should be at a higher level:

- What workflows are currently manual that shouldn't be?
- What integrations exist that are not being fully utilised?
- Where is the team spending time on work that automation could eliminate?

The goal is to position this as professional automation consulting — not a basic tool setup — and to demonstrate enough sophistication that the engagement feels like a genuine partnership.

---

## Differentiated Positioning

For this client, the pitch is:

> "I build automation systems that are designed to scale. Not just a bot that replies — a structured system with error handling, logging, conditional logic, and the ability to expand as your business grows."

## Discovery Questions

1. What automation tools are you currently using, if any?
2. Which workflows in the business are still manual and causing friction?
3. What does your customer or lead communication pipeline look like end-to-end?
4. Are there integrations between your existing tools that are missing?
5. What would a 10x version of your operations look like — and what's blocking it?
